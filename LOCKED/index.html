Our Company

Our Product

Towards the end of February of 2009, Jam Packed Inventions won a contract for its first big name product from Richard Carback. The contract was to develop a piece of software to act as a personal medical database to safely store and organize one and one's family's personal medical information. By March of 2009, JPI had gotten a laid our contract listing the specified requirements and developed a design for their aply named HealthPack system.
